    📝 Adding a Task:
    > Enter your task in the input box.
    > Select a date and time.
    > Click "Add" to save the task.

    ✏️ Editing a Task:
    > Click "Edit" to modify a task.
    > The details load into the input fields.
    > Click "Add" to update.

    ✅ Completing a Task:
    > Click on a task to mark it as done.
    > Completed tasks get dimmed.

    ❌ Deleting a Task:
    > Click "Delete" to remove a task.

    📅 Sorting Tasks:
    > Click "Sort" to arrange incomplete tasks by time.
    > Completed tasks stay at the bottom.
